# Unit 6 Reflection

In Unit 6, I learned how feedback and iteration play a key role in the design thinking process, especially during the testing phase. I practiced adding and styling different types of links, and polished web pages using favicons, image styling, and span tags. Most importantly, I learned how to gather and respond to client feedback, which helps me improve my work based on real input rather than just my own assumptions. This unit made me see that a good website isn't just built — it’s refined through collaboration and small, continuous improvements.
