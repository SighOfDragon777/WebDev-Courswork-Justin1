# Unit 5 Reflection
In this unit, I learned that CSS is essential for controlling the look and feel of a webpage, including font size, font family, colors, and backgrounds. I also explored the three ways to apply CSS — inline, internal, and external — and how they cascade to determine which styles take effect. Understanding these basics helps me separate content from design and build more structured, visually appealing websites.
